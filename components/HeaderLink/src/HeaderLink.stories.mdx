# @doc-blocks/header-link

The `HeaderLinks` and `HeaderLink` components are useful to link to documents outside of your storybook.
We use it to link to design specs.

You can have just one link in the header.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Single">
    <div>
      <HeaderLinks>
        <HeaderLink to="google.com">Google</HeaderLink>
      </HeaderLinks>
      <h1 style={{ display: 'inline' }}>The Header</h1>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>

Or even multiple.

<Preview>
  <Story name="Multiple">
    <div>
      <HeaderLinks>
        <HeaderLink to="amazon.com">Amazon</HeaderLink>
        <HeaderLink to="facebook.com">Facebook</HeaderLink>
        <HeaderLink to="google.com">Google</HeaderLink>
      </HeaderLinks>
      <h1 style={{ display: 'inline' }}>The Header</h1>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>
